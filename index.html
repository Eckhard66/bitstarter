Thinking. Your business on one page.

The MoodTracker App supports the patient-physician communication and relationship via an intuitive interface. It captures current situation, feelings, needs and wishes. The app captures and tracks the patient's self assessment about his health and helps to make the most out of the visit with the attending doctor. The intuitive user interface enables any patient - independent of education - to communicate the necessary. The app can be used by the patient while in hospital and while at home: While waiting for an appointment with the doctor, the patient can capture how she/he feels (about nutrition, pain, fear, treatments, side-effects,..) without words, just by swiping over the different topics ranked by smileys While at home, the app can be used to capture patient mood daily and when send to the treating physician - to evaluate the with the option to call in an appointment or an other required intervention. Secured by mobile two-factor authentication, the patient-centered application offers the highest privacy since the patient owns the data and gives access to the data shared; the physician can easily integrate the patient data into existing EMR of the health facility.

The PLUS for the Physician: ??????.

Increased patient satisfaction: The empowered patient asking the right questions is the more happy one More new patients: More referrals from happy, established patients to potential new patients Increased capacity and saving time: The physician can capture the patient's real situation faster, and can focus and respond on the need Continuous care and instant changes: The physician can instantly adapt the ongoing activities for the patient (e.g. with reduction of treatment, increase of pain medication, additional consultation with psychiatrist; new nutrition concept,..). The app would be connected to the health facilities pharmacy and nurse stations to automatically change the medication/pain/nutrition dosages in receipts and in the medical records. Our Journey: just starting

SO FAR

Over the past three years we have built several prototypes and tested them heavily. Constantly, we have revised and improved the design and mechanicsK

NOW

Our prototype holds internally the fully functioning and patented mechanical unit. Also, the size and shape resemble the final product that will enter production. We have designed the housing of our prototypes virtually and have produced half a dozen using 3D printers and tested them heavily. We were supplied with Texas Bluetooth Smart Module and are very satisfied with its performance. The mechanical unit, the housing and the Bluetooth Smart Module comply with our high production standards. Instruments

Future Steps

Your financial support will allow us to invest in a high-performance molding tool, built after our specifications. This molding tool will be used to produce the housing via injection molding. Given that the mechanical units require the precision and quality of a clock work, they will be produced with the use of a CNC lathe. The programming of the Bluetooth Smart Modules and for the App will be further optimized by our engineers. Final assembly of each KEEO will be done by hand, running through extensive quality checks before leaving our facilities.
